# UHC Scenario List

## Legende

| Symbol | Bedeutung |
|--------|-----------|
| **(H)** | High impact – starker Einfluss auf das Spielgeschehen |
| **(L)** | Low impact – geringer Einfluss auf das Spielgeschehen |
| **(Not comb.)** | Kann nicht mit anderen Szenarien kombiniert werden |
| 🔴 **Admin Scen** | Nur mit Admin-Erlaubnis hostbar |



## Tournament Scenarios

### Iron Kit
You will receive iron armor along with an iron sword, fishing rod, bow, 3 golden apples and 48 arrows to fight against your opponent!

### UHC Kit
You will be iron gear with Projectile Protection 2 and Protection 2. You will have 4 golden apples and 2 golden heads. You will also have an iron sword with sharpness one and a bow with power 1, and a stack and a half of arrows (96 arrows).

### UHC+ Kit
You will receive Protection 2 Diamond helmet, Diamond Chest-plate, and Diamond boots. You will also receive Projectile Protection 3 Iron leggings. You will have 2 stacks of arrows, along with a Sharpness 2 Diamond sword, and a power 2 bow. For your healing, you will have 6 golden apples and 3 golden heads.

### Archer Kit
This will test your bow skills. You will receive an Infinity bow with 1 arrow. You will be wearing leather armor with Projectile Protection for this kit.

## UHC Scenarios

### 1.7 Enchants (L)
Enchanting is reverted to how it works in MC 1.7, where it takes all the levels it costs, you cannot preview what you are getting and no lapis is required. Anvils are still 1.8.

### 100 Hearts (H)
Everyone will start with 100 hearts! Golden apples will heal 20% of your max health.

### 9 Lives (H) 🔴 Admin Scenario
At the start of the game, you will have 10 hearts. Each time you die, you will be rescattered into the game, gain 2 golden apples, and lose 1 permanent heart. You will keep playing until you have 1 permanent heart remaining where if you die with 1 permanent heart left, you will no longer respawn into the game.

### Absorption Partner (L)
Whenever you eat a golden apple, your teammate will gain an absorption heart.

### Absorption Switch (L)
By left clicking on a golden apple, it will either switch to giving 2 hearts of absorption and 0 hearts of regeneration or a golden apple with heal 2 hearts without the absorption. Golden heads, when toggled to absorption on, will give out 2 hearts and heal whatever the host set the regeneration for golden heads (3–4 hearts). When absorption is toggled off, it will heal the normal health the golden head regeneration is set at.

### Achievement Hunters (H)
When you earn a certain achievement, you will either be rewarded or penalized for earning that achievement. `/alist` is the command. Heads for this game are disabled and only available through 2 achievements.

### Achievement Paranoia (L)
If a player earns a specific achievement in normal Minecraft, the achievement will show, with who earned it and where they earned it.

### Achievements (H)
When you earn a normal achievement in normal Minecraft, you will either gain more attack, walking speed (both in code), or gain 1 heart.

### Alone Together (L)
You are not able to see your own teammates.

### Altitude Anvils (L)
Anvils are only usable on surface (above y60).

### Amplified Terrain (H)
The terrain will have giant mountains to climb and navigate around.

### Anonymous (L)
Players will be disguised as an IGN (currently "Unknown") and players will not be able to tell who is who. The health on tab is disabled. The normal chat and death messages will contain the players in game.

### Anvil Arrows (L)
Whenever a player dies, a giant arrow of anvils will spawn in the sky above where a player has died. The anvils are affected by gravity.

### Anvil Progression (L)
Any time you kill a player, you will get 1 use of the anvil. The more kills you accumulate, the more uses of the anvil you can use.

### Apple & Flint Switch (L)
Roles of materials have been switched. Flint now drops from leaves and apples drop from gravel.

### Aquaphobia (H)
The world is an abandoned city with water rising from Y:32 to Y:80. Whenever you craft a helmet you will get Respiration 3 and Aqua Affinity and when you craft boots, you will get Depth Strider 3.

### Armageddon (L)
THE WORLD IS ENDING! Blocks and objects will fall from the sky, this will include: Gravel, Gold Nuggets, Splash potions that will damage you, lava, etc.

### Armor Compensation (L)
The amount of teammates remaining determines the number of diamond armor each teammate can wear. If you are a solo, you can wear full. Teams of 2: 3/4ths Diamond. Teams of 3: Half Diamond. Teams of 4: one piece diamond armor.

### Armor Reveal (L)
At the start of the game, your armor will not be shown to other players. Until you kill a player, your armor will be revealed towards other players.

### Assassins (H)
At PvP, you will receive a target. That is your target until the target dies, then it switches to another target. If you manage to kill your target, they will drop all of their loot. If you kill a player that is not your target, then nothing will drop but their head. You need to craft a Compass in order to track your target.

### Assault and Battery (H) *(Teams of 2 only!)*
1 player can do damage with melee and the other can do damage with ranged weapons. If your teammate dies, then you will be able to use melee and ranged weapons.

### Astrophobia (H)
Meteors will fall from the world creating craters and exploding the world. It will leave a hole in the earth and if you're lucky, it'll leave 2 gold or a diamond in the crater. The creepers will now be supercharged and deal more damage and explode faster. If you manage to kill one, there is a possibility to receive their spawn egg. The skeletons are the aliens and will shoot their "lasers" and it'll deal more damage. It will always be night time, not day time.

### Australia (H, not comb.) 🔴 Admin Scenario
In this gamemode, each individual will be set as a different animal that lives in the continent of Australia. For more information about the animals and their powers, use the command `/scen` and click on the scenario called Australia during a game where it's enabled.

### Backpacks (L)
You will have the ability to open your backpack (`/bp`) to gain an extra 27 inventory slots for yourself. If you die with items in your backpack, they will fall to the floor.

### BAFB (L)
Stands for "Block at First Break". Breaking your first block will add you to a team with other people who broke the same block as you. Once a team is full, anyone trying to break a similar block as you will not be added to a team. Once you are added to a team, you cannot leave a team.

### Balance (L)
After mining 8 diamonds, it will be harder to mine more diamonds. After mining 48 gold, it will be harder to mine more gold.

### Balance+ (L)
Players are allowed to mine a total of 8 diamonds and 48 gold. Whenever you kill a player, you can mine 2 more diamonds and 8 more gold.

### Bald Chicken (L)
Chickens do not drop feathers in this scenario. Instead, skeletons will drop 10–20 arrows upon their death.

### Barebones (H)
Your best tier of mining is Iron, you cannot mine Gold or Diamonds, or obtain any anvils or enchant tables. If you kill a player, they will drop 1 diamond, a golden apple, 2 string and 32 arrows.

### Bats (L) 🔴 Admin Scenario
Whenever you kill a bat there is a 99% chance that you get 1 Golden Apple and a 1% chance to be instantly dead.

### Battle Royale (H) 🔴 Admin Scenario
You are allowed to team up with anyone but only 1 will win. All skeleton, spider, and chicken drop rates have been decreased by 50%. Cobwebs will only drop string 10% of the time. You cannot talk in chat or message players, and no death messages will show. Every 20 minutes, you will get a notification in chat as to who has died. Whenever a player dies, their body will be on the floor of where they died.

### Battle Siren (L)
Whenever a player starts fighting another player, it will show the general coordinates of where the fight is currently happening.

### Bed Bombs (L)
When you place down a bed and right click it, the bed will explode. It'll explode in every world (Overworld, Nether, and End).

### BestPvE (L)
Every 10 minutes, if you have not taken damage yet, you will gain a heart. If you take damage, then you are off the list. The only way to get back onto the list is to kill a player.

### Beta 1.7 Terrain (H)
The terrain will be based off of the generated terrain in the version Beta 1.7.

### Beta Zombies (L)
Instead of zombies dropping Rotten Flesh, instead they drop 0–2 feathers.

### Big Crack (L)
The Big Crack is located at X:0 or Z:0 and is a giant crack to the void with a 25 block radius.

### Biome Bundle (H)
The map is generated with custom terrain and custom structures from the mod Biome Bundle.

### Biome Enchanters (H)
You are allowed to enchant 1 item per biome. (e.g. If you enchant your sword in a plains biome, you cannot enchant another item in the plains biome.)

### Biome Paranoia (L)
On tab your name has a color that represents the biome you are in. To check the biomes, use `/bl`.

### Birds (H) 🔴 Admin Scenario
All players are allowed to fly in the game. Fall damage will be disabled.

### Black Plague (H)
Over time, the land becomes deceased and dead. It kills off any passive mob and gives hostile mobs a buff. Silverfish spawn naturally in the deceased area. While you are in the dead land, all the healing that you heal with will be halved. If you get hit by an aggressive mob in the dead land, there is a chance the negative effect gets to you for a certain time. If you have this negative effect and shoot/melee another player, they too will receive the negative effect.

### Blast Mining (H)
Whenever you mine an ore, even nether quartz, there is a 5% chance that a creeper will spawn on you. There is also a 3% chance of ignited TNT spawning on you.

### Bleeding Some Decent Items (L)
Whenever a player dies, they will drop a flower pot, some gold nuggets, sticks, and an Efficiency 2 Iron pickaxe.

### Bleeding Sweets (L)
Whenever a player dies, they will drop a diamond, 5 gold, a book, 1 string, 16 arrows.

### Blind Dates (H)
You start off as a team of 2, but at PvP, you will receive a wool, banner, or a firework. If you find a team of 2 with that same color, then you are on the same team!

### Block Battle (L)
Every 10 minutes, the player with the most unique blocks in their inventory will win 4 diamonds. If it's a tie, no one wins!

### Block Rush (L)
Whenever you mine a different type of block, you will be rewarded with 1 gold ingot.

### Blood Brothers (H) *(Teams of 2 only!)*
Whenever you eat a gap, head, or health potion, it will heal your teammate instead of you.

### Blood Cycle (H)
Every 10 minutes the Blood Cycle will target an ore that has a chance to make you lose half a heart. The cycle of ores is random and includes: Coal, Iron, Gold, Redstone, Lapis, Emerald, and Diamond. If you're lucky, the game will have a "no ore," meaning no ores will deal half a heart to you.

### Blood Diamonds (L)
Every time you mine a diamond ore, you will lose half a heart.

### Blood Diamonds+ (L)
Whenever you mine a diamond, you will take 1% of damage or 0.1 hearts. The damage will progressively continue for each diamond you mine.

### Blood Enchants (L)
Every time you enchant an item, you will lose half a heart.

### Blood Lapis (H) 🔴 Admin Scenario
Whenever you mine lapis ore, you will lose 0.5 hearts. The lapis will only drop 1–2 lapis.

### Blood Market (L)
The first 8 players bid for players using heart points. The remaining hearts the owner has left will be their max health throughout the game.

### Blood Market Double Packaging (L)
Same as Blood Market, but owners bid for 2 players at once (randomized pairs).

### Blood Market Quad Packaging (L)
Same as Blood Market, but owners bid for 4 players at once (randomized groups).

### Bombers (H)
Everybody will have an unbreakable Flint and Steel, and most animals and monsters/mobs that you kill will drop Dynamite/TNT.

### Bookception (L)
Players will drop a random enchanted book on death.

### BowFighters (H)
Everybody will start off with an infinity book, an arrow and two string. Your most powerful weapon can be a stone axe or wooden sword.

### Bowless (L)
Bows are disabled! You cannot craft, use, nor obtain bows!

### Breakup (L)
Before the start of the game, players on a big team will be separated into smaller teams.

### Cannibalism (L)
Whenever you eat a player head, you will gain 1 empty heart. This heart will be permanent and will not go away.

### Captains (L)
The captains are either picked by the host or chosen randomly and they pick players that they want on their team. (Order is snake draft: 1,2,3,4,4,3,2,1.)

### Carrot Combo (H)
Whenever you craft a sword, it'll give you a carrot with the equivalent attack damage that sword has.

### Catacombs (H, not comb.)
There will be several layers underground that replicate the surface in the game!

### Chameleons (L)
Based on what Minecraft skin you're wearing, you will be disguised in that biome.

### Cherry World (L, 1.21+ only)
The entire world is a Cherry Blossom biome!

### Children Left Unattended (L)
Whenever a teammate dies, each player on that team earns a speed potion and a tamed wolf.

### Chump Charity (L)
Every 10 minutes, the person who is on the lowest health gets fully healed.

### Chunk Apocalypse (H, not comb.)
Every chunk within the UHC world has a 10% chance of replacing EVERY block with an air block. (You can fall into the void!)

### Chunk Biomes (H)
Each chunk has its separate biome. These chunks have slime blocks on the edges to separate the biomes from its chunk.

### Circus (L)
When you kill a mob, there's a 25% chance the spawn egg for that mob will drop.

### City World (H)
Instead of a normal UHC world, the whole world is replaced with a City, meaning Mineshafts, Buildings, Sewers, etc.

### Compensation (H)
When a teammate dies, their total health is divided to the remaining teammates. Golden Apples heal 20% of your current total health, while Golden Heads heal 40% of your health.

### Corpses (L)
When a player dies, their corpse will remain on the ground to show where they died.

### Corrupt Kings (H)
Every team will have a king. The king will be unknown to the team. Each time a teammate dies, the king gets their hearts. The king will have 20 hearts and strength once all of their teammates have died.

### Craftable Absorption (L)
Absorption is off by default. When you surround a golden apple with 4 more gold, it will become an Absorption Golden Apple.

### Craftable TP (L)
Everyone in the game will start with 1 ender pearl. By renaming the ender pearl with the exact IGN of a player that is alive in the game, once you throw the ender pearl, you will be teleported 50 blocks away from the player's location.

### CraftClean (L)
In a crafting table, if you place coal or charcoal and surround the fuel with any smeltable item, you will get its smelted result.

### Creeper Pong (H)
Players start with a stack of Charged Creeper Spawn Eggs, a Knockback X stick and an Unbreaking X Flint and Steel.

### Cryophobia (H, not comb.)
You will spawn in an icy/snowy world. Skeletons will wear leather armor and shoot snowballs. As the game progresses, the ice rises. The ice will start at Y:0 at the start of the game and will continue to rise, stopping when meetup is called or when the ice reaches the surface.

### Cupid (L)
Every time you shoot and hit a player with your bow, you gain 1% of your health back.

### Custom 0,0 (H)
The map at 0,0 will be customized by the host!

### CutClean (L)
All ores and animal food will drop in their smelted version.

### Damage Cycle (H)
Every 10 minutes, the damage cycle will change. If you take that same type of damage from what the cycle said, you will take 3 times the damage. To check the damage type you need to avoid, do `/current`.

### Dead Biomes (L)
Every 10 minutes, a new biome will be announced as a "Dead Biome". If players are in that biome, they will receive Slowness 1 and Weakness 1 and for every minute they stay in the biome, they will lose 1 heart.

### Deep Diggers (L, 1.21+ only, not comb. with other potion effect scenarios)
Players that are under Y:-32 will receive Haste 1.

### Demolition (H)
Most of the ores/blocks have been replaced with TNT. As you decrease your Y level, more TNT will spawn in more veins and frequencies. If you place down TNT, it will instantly ignite it. There is also no bedrock in the world so you can fall into the void!

### Depths (L)
As your Y level goes down, the monsters will get stronger and stronger.

### Desert Flat World (H, not comb.)
The world is a desert superflat world with very high village & temple rates. Villager spawning is disabled. You start with 20 villager spawn eggs. Enchant tables will automatically have lapis in them.

### Diamond Limit (L)
Players can only mine a certain amount of diamonds. The limit can be viewed with `/diamondlimit` and is customizable by the host.

### Diamondless (H)
When you mine a diamond, the diamond will not drop; instead a piece of iron will drop. No diamonds will be dropped when a player dies.

### Dolphins (L)
If you swim in the water, your swim speed increases. You cannot drown in the water.

### DoNotDisturb (L)
Whenever the border reaches ±100 radius or ±200 diameter, if you hit a player, it will lock you with that player or team for 15 seconds. When you kill a player, you will be able to safely get the items for 60 seconds.

### DoNotDisturb+ (L)
When the border reaches ±250 radius or ±500 diameter, hitting a player will lock you in combat with that player for 15 seconds. When a player dies, their loot will be locked to the killer for 30 seconds.

### Doomsday Clock (H)
There is a nuclear doomsday clock that starts at X number of seconds to midnight. Every time a player dies, the doomsday clock will move one second closer to midnight. Upon reaching midnight, the game will become permanight and squids will begin to fall from the sky and explode 5 seconds after landing.

### Double Big Crack (L)
Upon the X:0 and Z:0 coordinates will lie 2 giant cracks that go all the way to the border. Beware of the void!

### Double Dates (L)
You and another player will be paired up randomly with another team of 2 before the start of the game.

### Double Healing (H)
All golden apples, golden heads, and instant health pots will heal twice as much.

### Double Health (L)
Everyone will start with 20 hearts! Golden apples will heal 20% of your max health.

### Double Moles (H)
Instead of one mole on the team, there will now be 2 moles on your team to worry about.

### Double or Nothing (L)
Whenever you mine iron, gold, emerald, or diamonds, there is a 50% chance that you get 2 ores, or you get nothing!

### Double Slimy Crack (L)
Coordinates of X:0 and Z:0 will have 2 cracks that go to the border, with slime blocks underneath to break your fall.

### Drafters (L)
This is a captains mode that instead of picking people freely, you're restricted from picking from a list of 5 people at random. Same captains rules apply.

### Dragon Rush (H)
At 0,0 lays a portal to the end. But 3 eyes of ender are missing from the portal. The goal is to race and defeat the dragon!

### Drop (L)
Every player starts the game at 0,0. For the first 5 minutes you can use `/drop <Quadrant>` to be teleported to the 500,500 coordinates of that quadrant and gain speed 2 for 5 minutes.

### Drought (H) 🔴 Admin Scenario
The world is a never-ending drained ocean with some patches of TNT underground. You will start with a water bucket. The only mobs that can spawn are creepers, which have a chance to drop bones, string, and arrows.

### Dungeoneering (H) 🔴 Admin Scenario
Ore rates have been dropped except redstone, lapis, and emerald. Dungeons in caves will spawn more frequently. There is no bedrock and TNT veins spawn. If you place the TNT it will automatically ignite.

### Eggs (H)
You start the game with 3 eggs and when you throw them any mob/animal in the game can come out of the egg. You can get more eggs from chickens when they lay them.

### Eight Legged Freaks (H) 🔴 Admin Scenario
All hostile mobs will be replaced with Spiders with different abilities and powers.

### Elements (H, not comb.) ⚠️ BROKEN
Each player will get an ability: either water, earth, fire, and air.

### Enchant Paranoia (L) 🔴 Admin Scenario
The enchant preview will be disabled. When you enchant an item, it will be enchanted with the enchantment name "???".

### Enchant Progression (L)
All players receive 1 enchanting table use for each kill they get.

### Enchanted Death (L)
You cannot craft enchanting tables. When a player dies, they will drop an enchanting table.

### End Meetup (L)
Once the game has reached its Meetup phase, players will be frozen and teleported into the end. After 10 mins, the dragon will spawn if the game is not completed.

### Ender Dance (H)
Race to be on top of one of the dragon's healing pillars, destroy it, then place a jukebox with a music disk on one of the pillars to win the game!

### Enemy Recon (L)
Whenever you get a kill you will earn 1 recon, allowing you to spy on a player's inventory using `/er <Player>`. It has a 30% chance of broadcasting in chat who you are spying on.

### Experienced Crafter (H)
If you want to craft a certain item, it will cost you levels to craft it.

### Extreme Mobs (H, not comb.)
Various rare mob variants can spawn, including Giant Zombies (1%), Creeper Jockeys (5%), fire-spreading creepers (10%), silverfish-spawning creepers (10%), and flying witches (50%).

### Fallout (H)
If you are not below Y:60 after 45 minutes in the game, you will take half a heart every 30 seconds you stay on the surface.

### Fast Getaway (L)
Whenever you kill a player, you will gain 45 seconds of speed 2. If you get hit or hit another player, you will lose the Speed.

### Fast Smelting (L)
Items in the furnace will only take 1 second to smelt per item.

### Feather Boots (L)
When you craft leather boots using feathers instead of leather, they will have Feather Falling 4 upon crafting.

### Fireless (L)
All fire damage (includes lava and fire swords) will not do damage to you.

### Fissures (H)
Every 10 minutes, an earthquake will happen somewhere on the map, creating a jagged line with lava on its surface. If you are within 50 blocks of a fissure, you will take 1 heart of damage and gain nausea and slowness temporarily.

### Flat World (H, not comb.)
The world will be completely flat with its surface at Y:5. Village rates will be upped.

### Flatlands (L)
The entire world's surface is at Y:63 in all biomes, resulting in a flat world that still has caves and normal biomes/structures.

### Flippers (L)
When you craft golden boots, they will automatically have Depth Strider 3.

### Flooded (H)
ALL of the world's air blocks between Y:32 to Y:70 will all be covered in water. Players will receive relevant enchantment books, some sugar cane, a couple of spawn eggs, food, leather, and feathers. Players will also receive permanent Water Breathing and Night Vision effects.

### Flower Power (H, not comb.)
Whenever you break a flower, you will gain a random item (up to a stack of 64, 32, or 16 depending on the item). You will have 30 seconds to pick it up before it despawns.

### Food Consumption (H, not comb.) ⚠️ Must be hosted with Lapis Consumption
Whenever you eat a certain piece of food, you will gain a certain potion effect.

### Football Edition (H, not comb.)
Each player on a team will be given a specific position (Goalkeeper, Defender, Midfielder, or Forward) with different powers.

### Fortune Babies (H)
Every tool that you craft will have Fortune 1.

### Fortune Boys (H)
Every tool that you craft will have Fortune 2.

### Fortune Men (H)
Every tool that you craft will have Fortune 3.

### Fragile Spirits (L)
Whenever a player dies on surface, it will spawn an ocelot with Speed 1 and Resistance 1. If they die underground, it will spawn a bat with Resistance 2. If you kill these animals, you will gain the dead player's head.

### Frozen in Time (H)
All current movement in water and lava will not flow. Gravel, sand, cacti, and sugarcane will be unaffected by gravity. Leaves will not decay. Your only way of getting apples is through shearing leaves.

### Game Health (L)
The health on tab shown on a player's name will be the combined health of every player alive during the game.

### Game Inventory (L)
Everyone in the game has access to `/gi` to share items between every player in the game.

### Gap Zap (L)
If you take damage while you are regenerating from a golden apple or head, the regeneration cancels.

### Gapple Roulette (L)
Whenever you eat a Golden Apple, you will get a random potion effect for a certain time.

### Generous Admin (H)
Every so often, a host or staff member will be scattered in the world with randomly selected goodies. You will receive a compass tracking them and if you kill them, you receive the goodies.

### Genie (L)
You are given 3 wishes and you cannot get more. Based on the number of kills you have determines what you can wish for.

### Go To Hell (H, not comb.)
After a certain time period (45–60 minutes into the game), players will proceed into the Nether. If the player is not in the nether, they will take 0.5 hearts every 30 seconds.

### Golden Pearl (L)
Surrounding a pearl with 8 gold in a crafting table will create a Golden Pearl. Golden Pearls will deal no damage when used.

### Golden Retriever (L)
Whenever a player dies, instead of the normal head post, it will drop a Golden Head.

### Goldless (L)
Whenever you mine a piece of gold, it will drop a piece of iron instead. When a player dies, they will drop a golden head and 8 gold.

### Gold Rush (H)
You cannot craft any leather armor or iron armor. You can only craft either Gold or Diamond Armor.

### Gone Fishing (H)
At the start of the game, players receive 20,000 levels, 64 anvils, and an Unbreaking 250 and Luck of the Sea 250 fishing rod. Players cannot craft the enchantment table.

### Good Game (L)
The first player to say "gg." after a player dies will receive a reward. Players who type toxic messages will receive negative effects.

### Grave Robbers (L)
Whenever a player dies, their stuff will be in a grave with a double chest.

### Grinch (L)
Every Midnight at 0,0 there is a zombie known as the "Grinch". If you kill him, you will receive the present of whatever the Grinch was holding.

### Halloween (H, Only Active on Oct. 31st)
Random lightning strikes, bats spawn around you, getting hit by a hostile mob causes 15 seconds of blindness, all spiders are replaced with cave spiders, witch rates are upped, zombies and skeletons spawn with jack o' lanterns.

### Hastey Babies (L)
Every tool that you craft will have Efficiency 1.

### Hastey Boys (L)
Every tool that you craft will have Efficiency 3 and Unbreaking 1.

### Hastey Getaway (L)
Whenever you kill a player, you will gain Haste 2 for 45 seconds.

### Hastey Men (L)
Every tool that you craft will have Efficiency 5 and Unbreaking 3.

### Head Hunters (L)
When a player dies, their head will be somewhere 75 blocks out from where they died, on the surface.

### Health Donor (L)
Players can donate health to other players. Every half a heart you donate costs 1 experience level. Command: `/donatehp <1-20> <IGN>`.

### Hex Lands (L)
The world is now using hexagonal terrain generation, with each hexagon being a different biome with a stone brick wall separating the hexagons.

### Homework (H) ⚠️ BROKEN
Every 10 minutes, you will be assigned an item that you must collect and have in your inventory.

### Ice Cage (L)
Whenever a player dies, a sphere of ice will spawn around where that player has died.

### Identity Reveal (L)
All players start out the game with a disguise. When a player gets a kill, their true identity is revealed to everyone.

### Identity Theft (L) ⚠️ BROKEN
Whenever you shoot and hit a player, you will gain their disguise.

### Infestation (H, not comb.)
Whenever a monster dies, it has a 40% chance to respawn where it died.

### Infinite Allies (H)
You can have as many allies as you want, but only 1 man can win.

### Infinite Enchanter (H)
At the start of the game, everyone will receive 2 stacks of Bookshelves, anvils and enchant tables, and infinite levels.

### Inventors (L)
The first person to craft something will be broadcasted in the chat.

### Inventors+ (L)
The first player to craft something will be broadcasted in the chat. They will either receive 1 diamond or 2 gold.

### Inverted Dimensions (H, not comb.)
The nether and the overworld have been switched. You cannot place water in the overworld.

### Inverted Parallel (H, not comb.)
When you go underneath Y:42 in the overworld, it will be replaced by the "nether." You cannot place water in the "nether."

### Investments (L)
You can invest diamonds and gold into a player. If the player gets a kill, you receive double the amount. If they die, you lose your investment.

### Islands (H, not comb.)
There are 10-wide cracks running along the x and z axis every 100 blocks.

### Kill Reveal (L)
The teams will be secret until players get a kill. Once they get a kill, their color is shown on the tab list.

### Kings (L)
Upon a team, 1 person will be King with Strength, Resistance, Haste, and 20 hearts. If the king dies, the remaining teammates will receive weakness for 3 mins and Poison for 15 seconds.

### Krenzinator (L)
9 redstone blocks in a crafting table = 1 diamond. Crafting a diamond sword costs 1 heart. Throwing an egg that hits a player does half a heart. Horses and the nether are disabled.

### Lapis Consumption (H, not comb.) ⚠️ Must be hosted with Food Consumption
Whenever a player mines a lapis lazuli ore, it will drop a random food item.
